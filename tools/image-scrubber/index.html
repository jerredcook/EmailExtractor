ImageScrubber

Strip hidden metadata (EXIF, GPS location, camera info) from photos and optionally resize/compress — entirely in your browser, nothing uploaded.

1Drop images JPEG / PNG / WebP
↑
Click or drag one or more images here
(.jpg, .png, .webp, … accepts any image)
How it works: each image is decoded to raw pixels, painted onto a <canvas>, then re-encoded. Because the output is built from pixels alone, every embedded marker is dropped — EXIF, GPS coordinates, camera make/model, timestamps, thumbnails and color profiles do not survive the re-encode.
2Options applied to all
0.85

Resizing only scales down — images smaller than the limit are never upscaled. Aspect ratio is preserved.

    Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored.
