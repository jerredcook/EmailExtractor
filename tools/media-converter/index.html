The FFmpeg engine (~31 MB, single-threaded WebAssembly) is vendored into this tool and downloads once on your first conversion, then stays cached. Everything runs locally — no upload, no account. Conversions are slower than native FFmpeg, so large videos can take a while.
Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored.
