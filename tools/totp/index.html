Privacy — read this
Entries live only in this page's memory and vanish when you reload or close the tab. That is deliberate — nothing is written to disk, localStorage, cookies, or anywhere else.
  • Codes are computed locally with WebCrypto (HMAC-SHA-1/256/512). No network requests; works offline.
  • Great for testing 2FA during development, setting up a service, or reading a code off a secret you already legitimately have.
  • A phone authenticator app is the right home for long-term secrets — this page intentionally forgets everything.
  • Anyone who can see a TOTP secret can generate its codes forever. Treat secrets like passwords; clear your clipboard after pasting one.
Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored.
