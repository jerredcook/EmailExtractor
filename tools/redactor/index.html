Redactor

Black out or pixelate the sensitive parts of a screenshot or document photo — the pixels are destroyed, not covered, and the image never leaves your browser.

1Load an image
▣
Drop an image here or click to browse
JPEG, PNG or WebP — processed entirely in this tab
2Drag rectangles to redact

Redactions are irreversible by design. Each committed rectangle overwrites the image pixels — the data is destroyed, not covered by a layer, so it cannot be undone by an attacker or by you. Undo only replays the last 10 steps of this session from in-memory snapshots; the exported file always contains only the baked pixels.

3Download

The image is re-encoded from the canvas, which also strips all EXIF/GPS metadata as a side effect.

Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored.
